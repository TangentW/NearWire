1,440 by 900 windows in light and dark appearances.

Inspection confirmed:

- the persistent TLS and Bonjour discovery captions are absent;
- the compact Pairing Code label, prominent monospaced code, listener state, Copy, Refresh, and
  Pause/Resume actions occupy the leading side;
- Performance and panel controls occupy the trailing side of the title row;
- new-device approval occupies the far right of the pairing and connection-action row;
- no header control is clipped at the maintained minimum size;
- the expanded Composer occupies exactly 240 points, shows the complete horizontal send form, and
  stays inside the window in both appearances;
- validation feedback retains usable text inputs and send feedback owns a bounded internal scroll
  region;
- the analysis and Composer regions do not overlap;
- there is no draggable horizontal divider for changing Composer height.
