## Cross-Layer Production Evidence

`SDKSessionAdmissionTests.testRealTLSProductionChannelCompletesAdmissionSequence` uses the production Viewer listener and App channel over loopback TLS without Bonjour. It proves the permanent admission handoff, policy activation, an uplink Event, a downlink Event, queue drain, and explicit terminal teardown. Deterministic mailbox backpressure is proved by `testTransportBlockWithWholeTokensDoesNotPollUntilCapacityProgress` and the secure-mailbox reservation suite because operating-system socket buffering is intentionally not used as a race oracle.
